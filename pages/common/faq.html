Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register as a farmer?

A: You can register as a farmer by clicking on the 'Create Account' link and selecting the farmer account type during registration.

Q: How can I sell my products on AgroConnect?

A: Once registered as a farmer, you can add your products through your farmer dashboard. Make sure to provide clear descriptions and images.

Q: Is there a fee to use AgroConnect?

A: Basic accounts are free. Premium features or transaction fees may apply for certain services. Please refer to our Terms of Service for details.
